Northern European Cyclist Tour, Print

The production of this map was a valuable journey working with satellite raster data in and out of Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. The goal was to enhance the landscape for a bicycle race promotional (tourist) map. Dealing with vertical exaggeration, assigning a land cover type pixel to match the elevation data, generalizing lines, and mixing raster graphics with vector graphics, the Northern European Cyclist Tour was a handful, but produced a satisfying result of a picturesque landscape. This map, route, and event were created entirely for learning and were not used in promotional event. Additionally, this map was specifically designed for print production and not originally intended for the web.
