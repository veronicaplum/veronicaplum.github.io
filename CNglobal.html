China's Global Involvement, Infographic

An array of perspectives on China's role in the world as it emerges as a global superpower. This open-ended narrative was completed in the form of a three part narrative infographic. The most difficult part of the is project was finding the data, which came in from multiple sources. Ment to inform for a general audience, it was difficult making careful decisions on which areas of global involvement to focus on. All icons and maps are my original work.
